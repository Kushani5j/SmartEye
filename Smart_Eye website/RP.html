Research Problem

There are approximately 39 million of blind people in the world. Independent mobility for visually impaired people is one of the main concerns when it comes to this topic. White canes are considered to be the mostly used accessory to help this course. But white canes have so many limitations. Namely, the range being small, not being able to detect obstacles above waist, sudden depths, and not being able to detect objects till the cane come contact with it. And also another problem for the blind is that they have no measure of their orientation relative to their path.
These problems takes out the confidence from the blind and restrict them from even getting out of their houses. Even though there have been researches done and attempts made to help the blind overcome these, there is no such device or a research attempt to give blind people a single device with which they can overcome these problems and help them with independent mobility.
