SMART EYE

Contemplating the problems that blinds face in day to day traveling, this proposal discusses the importance and impact of designing a useful equipment to improve the safety and confidence of the blind when they are traveling. Main intention is to deliver a wearable headset including cameras and sensors to identify obstacles and body vitals and also a mobile application to notify the sudden changes of the user to their relatives. Using Image Processing techniques identify the obstacles and the orientation of the user’s path. If it is a moving obstacle identify its direction and speed relative to the user. Our machine Learning algorithms identify users’ sudden changes of the body vitals. Intended inputs for the Image Processing algorithms would be the live video clips and for the Machine Learning algorithms would be the body vitals of the user. By analyzing the mentioned inputs, the system detects the obstacles in users’ path and give a voice notification to the user via headphones, identifies users’ sudden changes of the body, notifies the location of the user and the situation via mobile application to the relatives of the user. In order to perform the all mentioned outcomes, the data sets would be trained. The proposed solution will give a wearable headset that is capable of notifying obstacles in user’s path connecting with a mobile application. Which includes identifying stationary obstacles and moving obstacles, detecting sudden depths, identifying obstacles above the waist, detecting user’s orientation relative to the path and providing voice activated instructions required to maintain correct orientation, tracking and notifying about the user’s vitals and location.
